Russian Tea

A drink of my childhood, popular in the family since the craze in the 70s

Prep

  • 1 9oz jar Tang
  • ½ cup instant black tea or instant lemon iced tea
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp. cloves
  • Dash of salt

Let's do it

Mix together, add 2½-3 tsp. of mix to hot water.
