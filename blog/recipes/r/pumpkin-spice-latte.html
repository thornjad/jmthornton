Homemade Pumpkin Spice Latte

Make your own pumpkin spice latte at home

Prep

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 Tablespoons pureed pumpkin
  • 1½ Tablespoons sugar
  • ½ Tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice (additional to taste)
      Don't have pumpkin pie spice? Make your own!
    • 3 parts ground cinnamon
    • 2 parts ground ginger
    • 2 parts ground nutmeg
    • 1 large dash of allspice (optional)

Let's do it

  1. Simmer all ingredients together
  2. Mix with as many shots of espresso as you'd like, or simply pour into coffee
